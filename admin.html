Tournament Rules & Settings

Faan Scoring

Holds the tournament in a check-in phase before it officially starts: you pair Round 1 in advance and only lock it in once you know who showed up. When you Begin, anyone not checked in is dropped and Round 1 is condensed to fill the empty seats. Leave off to start immediately with the full roster.
After a chosen round, the top players split off into a separate finals bracket and play only each other for the rest of the tournament; their final placing is decided by the points they earn from the cut onward. Set how many players advance and which round the cut happens after.
If set to 0, cut triggers manually or immediately.

Regenerate Latest Round

Deletes the latest round's pairings and any scores entered for it, then re-pairs the current active players. Use this after withdrawals change who should be seated (e.g. to drop substitutes that are no longer needed).

Uses the bucket selection in the Generate Round section above — set it there before regenerating.
